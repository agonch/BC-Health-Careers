About.

The Bellevue College (BC) Health Careers Club is a student-run organization aimed to build and maintain bridges between students, medical professionals, and the local healthcare community.

This group intends to serve students in all grade levels (running start, undergrad, postbac, etc.) by providing opportunities to connect with each other, guest speakers, and prepare for future careers in different healthcare fields.
